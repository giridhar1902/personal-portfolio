# Cold Outreach Recipient Intelligence

Research repository studying what **recipients** of B2B cold outreach actually respond to, delete, and hate — not what senders claim works.

## The Angle

Everyone researches cold outreach senders and their tactics. This repo takes the opposite approach: it studies the recipients.

The 10 experts here are founders, VPs, and HR leaders who are bombarded with cold outreach daily and have publicly documented what works, what fails, and what gets them to reply. The goal is to build a recipient-validated playbook for an ATS company (100hires) whose customers send outreach to exactly these kinds of people.

## Why This Matters for 100hires

100hires sells to SMB hiring managers and founders. Their product includes AI email composer, candidate outreach sequences, and deliverability tools. These are not 100hires' users — they are 100hires' buyers. This playbook is for 100hires' internal SDRs, mapping the exact deletion triggers and cognitive friction of the SMB founders and TA leaders they cold pitch daily to sell ATS software.

## Repo Structure

```
cold-outreach-recipient-intelligence/
├── research/
│   ├── sources.md              # Annotated expert list and collection plan
│   ├── linkedin-posts/         # Raw posts and notes, one folder per expert
│   ├── youtube-transcripts/    # Video and podcast transcript excerpts
│   └── other/                  # Reddit threads, newsletter pulls
```

- **`research/sources.md`** — Who we're studying, why they matter, and where to find their content.
- **`research/linkedin-posts/`** — Per-expert folders for LinkedIn posts and commentary on cold outreach.
- **`research/youtube-transcripts/`** — Transcripts from talks, podcasts, and interviews.
- **`research/other/`** — Secondary sources: Reddit threads and newsletter excerpts.
- **`synthesis/`** — Distilled findings and product recommendations for 100hires.
