## Post 2

Date: 2025 (exact date unavailable)
URL: https://www.commsor.com/blog-categories/blog-go-to-network
Relevance: Introduces the "Go-to-Network" framework as a replacement for cold outbound.

Content:
Pipeline generation should bypass the cold inbox entirely during the initial phase. Instead of relying on passive intent data — which merely indicates that a prospect might have clicked a link — vendors must leverage network signals, which dictate exactly who within the vendor's ecosystem can provide a warm, trusted introduction. The modern B2B buyer possesses a deep desire to consult their peers, existing network, and trusted advisors before making any software procurement decisions. An effective outreach strategy does not attempt to brute-force a relationship through volume; it maps relational graphs to reduce friction through transferred trust.
