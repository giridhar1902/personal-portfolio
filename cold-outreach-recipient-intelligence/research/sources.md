# Research Sources

Working document. Each entry covers one expert studied as a **recipient** of cold outreach — not as a sender coach.

---

## Peep Laja

**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/peeplaja/

**Role & signal:** CEO of Wynter and CXL. Runs message-testing research with real buyers. His inbox is a lab — he documents why pitches fail from the recipient's side, backed by data, not theory.

**Recurring themes (recipient POV):**
- Generic "personalization" that references surface-level LinkedIn details gets ignored
- Buyers delete emails that lead with the sender's product instead of the recipient's problem
- Message-market fit matters more than subject-line hacks — recipients spot templated outreach instantly

**Best content format:** LinkedIn posts (frequent, detailed), Wynter blog posts, podcast interviews

## Andrew Gazdecki

## Dave Gerhardt

**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/davegerhardt/

**Role & signal:** Founder of Exit Five, former CMO. Spent years on both sides of B2B outreach. Now documents inbox fatigue and what makes a marketing leader reply from the receiving end.

- Recipients reward brevity, specificity, and proof the sender understands their world
