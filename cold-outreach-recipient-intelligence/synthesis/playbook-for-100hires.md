# Playbook for 100hires

Recipient-validated outreach guidance for 100hires customers.

## 1. What This Is

This is an inversion playbook for 100hires outbound SDRs. It maps the exact vendor deletion triggers and cognitive friction of the SMB founders and TA leaders you pitch daily. Use this to bypass their inbox defense mechanisms and reduce our internal CAC.

## 2. Key Findings

- Personalization must prove you understand their specific operational bottleneck, not just that you scraped their LinkedIn bio.
- AI-generated outreach is easily detected by technical leaders and instantly punished with a domain block.
- Attention spans are zero; if the core value proposition is not in the first sentence, the email is dead.
- Founders reject category-level tool pitches, demanding to know exactly how it impacts their bottom line or time-to-hire.
- Building basic social capital or network recognition dramatically increases the baseline cold reply rate.

## 3. What Gets You Deleted

- Fake rapport and pleasantries ("Hope you're having a great Thursday!").
- Using "synergy," "game-changer," or corporate buzzword bingo.
- Asking for 15 minutes of their time without earning the right to ask.
- Sending "Did you see my last email?" guilt-trip follow-ups.
- Dumping a feature list instead of pitching a measurable outcome.

## 4. What Gets a Reply

- Extreme brevity that forces you to respect their time (under 50 words).
- Calling out a specific, highly relevant pain point they are currently experiencing.
- Providing quantifiable proof of value (e.g., "reduced time-to-hire by 12 days").
- A low-friction, soft call-to-action ("Worth exploring?" or "Open to a chat?").
- Plain text formatting with zero HTML templates or unrequested calendar links.
- Pitching a concrete solution rather than a software category.

## 5. The One Rule

Respect their time more than you value your quota.
